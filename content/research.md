<section class="lab-panel lab-panel-muted">
  <p class="lab-eyebrow">Research Themes</p>
  <h2>Core directions</h2>
  <p>
    Our group develops electronic structure theory and numerical algorithms for
    realistic materials simulation across equilibrium and extreme conditions.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="lab-grid-2">
  <div class="lab-panel">
    <h3>Orbital-free and finite-temperature DFT</h3>
    <p>
      We design non-interacting free-energy and kinetic-energy functionals to enable
      large-scale simulations beyond conventional orbital-based approaches.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="lab-panel">
    <h3>Warm dense matter and planetary materials</h3>
    <p>
      We study transport, equation-of-state behavior, and structural properties of
      matter under high temperature and high pressure conditions.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="lab-grid-2">
  <div class="lab-panel">
    <h3>Reduced density matrix functional theory</h3>
    <p>
      We investigate one-body and two-body reduced density matrix formulations for
      strongly correlated electrons and improved exchange-correlation treatments.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="lab-panel">
    <h3>Numerical optimization and manifold methods</h3>
    <p>
      We develop direct minimization strategies, including Stiefel manifold methods,
      for robust and efficient Kohn-Sham and orbital-free workflows.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="lab-panel">
  <h3>Software and workflow interests</h3>
  <ul class="lab-list-compact">
    <li>High-throughput first-principles simulation pipelines</li>
    <li>Convergence diagnostics for pseudopotentials and basis sets</li>
    <li>Machine-learning-assisted molecular dynamics analysis</li>
    <li>Interoperable post-processing and reproducible data workflows</li>
  </ul>
</section>
